# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Guestify Media Kit Builder.

## General Questions

### What is Guestify Media Kit Builder?

Guestify Media Kit Builder is a tool that helps you create professional media kits through an easy-to-use drag-and-drop interface. It's designed for podcasters, speakers, authors, consultants, influencers, and anyone who needs to present their professional credentials and expertise.

### Who is Guestify for?

Guestify is perfect for:
- **Podcast guests** seeking more appearances
- **Speakers** looking for speaking engagements
- **Authors** promoting their books and expertise
- **Consultants** showcasing their services
- **Influencers** presenting their brand to partners
- **Entrepreneurs** building their personal brand

### Do I need technical skills to use Guestify?

No! Guestify is designed to be user-friendly with no coding or design skills required. If you can drag and drop, you can build a professional media kit.

### What's included in my subscription?

Your subscription includes:
- Media kit builder with all components
- AI-powered content generation tools
- Brand kit management
- Media gallery storage
- Pre-built templates and themes
- Publishing and sharing features

Specific limits vary by plan level.

## Media Kits

### How many media kits can I create?

This depends on your plan. Check your dashboard for your current limits.

### Can I duplicate a media kit?

Yes! In your media kit list:
1. Click the **...** menu on any media kit
2. Select **Duplicate**
3. A copy is created that you can customize

### Can I make my media kit private?

Yes. In your media kit settings, you can:
- Keep it as a draft (not published)
- Use a private link that only people with the link can access
- Password protect your media kit (on some plans)

### How do I share my media kit?

1. Publish your media kit
2. Copy your unique URL from the dashboard
3. Share via email, social media, or add to your website

### Can I embed my media kit on my website?

Yes! Get the embed code from your media kit settings and paste it into your website's HTML.

### What happens if I delete a media kit?

Deleted media kits are permanently removed along with their content. This cannot be undone. Consider exporting or duplicating before deleting.

## Components

### How do I add a component?

1. Open your media kit in the editor
2. Find the component in the left sidebar
3. Drag it onto your canvas
4. Drop it where you want it

### Can I reorder components?

Yes! Click and drag the handle (⋮⋮) on any component to move it up or down.

### How do I delete a component?

1. Click to select the component
2. Click the trash icon or press Delete
3. Confirm deletion

### Can I have multiple of the same component?

Yes, you can add multiple instances of most components (e.g., multiple testimonial sections).

### Are the AI tools free to use?

AI tools are included with your Guestify subscription. Some plans have usage limits.

Absolutely! AI-generated content is a starting point. You can:

## Brand Kits & Themes

### What's the difference between a theme and a brand kit?

- **Theme** = Pre-designed visual style (colors, typography, spacing)
- **Brand Kit** = Your custom colors that override theme defaults

You can use a theme for layout and styling, then apply your Brand Kit colors on top.

### How do I match my existing brand colors?

1. Create a new Brand Kit
2. Enter your hex color codes (e.g., #2563EB)
3. Save and link to your media kit

### Can I use multiple themes on one media kit?

No, each media kit uses one theme. However, different media kits can use different themes.

### Will changing my brand kit affect existing media kits?

Yes, if your media kits are linked to that Brand Kit. Changes automatically propagate.

## Media & Images

### What image formats are supported?

- JPG/JPEG
- PNG (including transparent backgrounds)
- GIF
- WebP
- SVG (for logos)

### What's the maximum file size?

- Images: 10MB
- Logos: 5MB
- Documents: 20MB

### What image size should I upload?

| Use Case | Recommended Size |
|----------|-----------------|
| Profile Photo | 500x500px minimum |
| Hero Background | 1920x1080px |
| Logos | 500px wide |
| Gallery Images | 1200px on longest side |

### Can I link to external images?

Yes, you can use image URLs from external sources, though uploading to your Media Gallery is recommended for reliability.

## Templates

### Can I switch templates after starting?

Yes, but switching templates changes your layout. Your content is preserved, but you may need to rearrange components.

### Can I create my own template?

Yes! Build a media kit the way you want, then save it as a custom template for future use.

### Will templates be updated?

We regularly add new templates and update existing ones. Updates won't affect your existing media kits.

## Publishing & Sharing

### Can I use my own domain?

Yes, on certain plans. Go to Settings > Custom Domain to configure.

### Is my media kit mobile-friendly?

Yes! All media kits are automatically responsive and look great on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

### Can I see who views my media kit?

Basic analytics are available showing views and visits. Advanced analytics may be available on higher plans.

### How do I unpublish a media kit?

1. Go to your media kit list
2. Click the **...** menu
3. Select **Unpublish**

Your media kit becomes a draft and is no longer publicly accessible.
