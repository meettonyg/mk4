# Components Overview

Components are the building blocks of your media kit. Each component serves a specific purpose and can be customized to match your brand and messaging.

## How Components Work

### Drag-and-Drop
1. Find the component you want in the left sidebar
2. Click and drag it onto your canvas
3. Drop it where you want it to appear
4. Click to select and edit

### Component Properties
Every component has:
- **Content** - The information displayed (text, images, links)
- **Settings** - Behavior and layout options
- **Design** - Visual customization (inherits from theme)

## Component Details

### Hero

The Hero component creates a prominent header section that immediately captures attention.

**Best for:** Opening your media kit with impact

**Fields:**
- Headline - Your main message or value proposition
- Subheadline - Supporting text
- Background image (optional)
- Call-to-action button

**Tips:**
- Keep your headline under 10 words
- Use action-oriented language
- Include a clear CTA

---

### Guest Intro

A concise introduction specifically designed for podcast hosts to read when introducing you.

**Best for:** Podcast guests who want hosts to have a ready-to-read intro

**Fields:**
- Introduction text (typically 50-100 words)
- Pronunciation guide (optional)
- Key talking points

**Tips:**
- Write in third person
- Include phonetic spelling if your name is unique
- Keep it under 30 seconds when read aloud

---

### Biography

Your professional story that establishes credibility and connection.

**Best for:** Sharing your background, experience, and journey

**Fields:**
- Short bio (50 words) - For social media and quick intros
- Medium bio (150 words) - For speaker introductions
- Long bio (300 words) - For detailed profiles

**Tips:**
- Use the AI Biography Generator for quick drafts
- Write in third person for professional contexts
- Include specific achievements with numbers

---

### Video Intro

Embed a video introduction to make a personal connection.

**Best for:** Adding personality and building rapport before meetings

**Fields:**
- Video URL (YouTube, Vimeo, or hosted)
- Thumbnail image (optional)
- Caption

**Tips:**
- Keep videos under 2 minutes
- Ensure good audio quality
- Look at the camera and smile

---

### Profile Photo

Your professional headshot that appears throughout your media kit.

**Best for:** Putting a face to your name

**Fields:**
- Image upload
- Alt text
- Shape (circle, square, rounded)

**Tips:**
- Use a high-quality, recent photo
- Ensure good lighting and a clean background
- Dress appropriately for your industry

---

### Company Logo

Display your business or organization's logo.

**Best for:** Establishing business credibility

**Fields:**
- Logo image
- Link URL (optional)
- Size settings

---

### Personal Brand Logo

Your personal brand mark or wordmark.

**Best for:** Creators and consultants with personal brands

**Fields:**
- Logo image
- Alt text

---

### Topics

Showcase the subjects you're an expert in.

**Best for:** Helping podcast hosts and event organizers understand what you can discuss

**Fields:**
- Topic title
- Description
- Icon (optional)

**Tips:**
- List 3-7 main topics
- Be specific rather than generic
- Include trending or timely topics when relevant

---

### Questions

Pre-written interview questions that hosts can ask you.

**Best for:** Making it easy for hosts to prepare great interviews

**Fields:**
- Question text
- Optional answer hints
- Category/grouping

**Tips:**
- Include 5-10 strong questions
- Mix easy and thought-provoking questions
- Avoid yes/no questions

---

### Topics-Questions

A combined component showing topics with related questions.

**Best for:** Comprehensive interview preparation for hosts

**Fields:**
- Topic
- Related questions for that topic

---

### Authority Hook

Your unique value proposition in a memorable format.

**Best for:** Quickly communicating who you help and how

**Fields:**
- WHO - Who do you help?
- WHAT - What result do you deliver?
- WHEN - When do they need you?
- HOW - How do you do it uniquely?

**Tips:**
- Use the AI Authority Hook Builder
- Be specific about your target audience
- Include measurable outcomes when possible

---

### Stats

Key numbers that demonstrate your impact and credibility.

**Best for:** Quick proof of your expertise and reach

**Fields:**
- Stat number
- Label/description
- Icon (optional)

**Examples:**
- "500+ clients served"
- "10M+ podcast downloads"
- "15 years experience"

---

### Testimonials

Quotes from clients, colleagues, or collaborators.

**Best for:** Building trust through social proof

**Fields:**
- Quote text
- Person's name
- Title/company
- Photo (optional)

**Tips:**
- Include specific results when possible
- Get testimonials from recognizable names in your industry
- Keep quotes concise and impactful

---

### Logo Grid

Display logos of publications, clients, or partners.

**Best for:** "As Seen In" or "Trusted By" sections

**Fields:**
- Logo images
- Links (optional)
- Grid layout settings

---

### Interviews

Showcase your past podcast or media appearances.

**Best for:** Demonstrating your experience as a guest

**Fields:**
- Show/publication name
- Episode title
- Link
- Date
- Logo/image

---

### Photo Gallery

A collection of professional images.

**Best for:** Providing high-res images for media use

**Fields:**
- Multiple images
- Captions (optional)
- Download settings

**Tips:**
- Include variety (headshots, action shots, speaking photos)
- Provide high-resolution images
- Consider including images with different backgrounds

---

### Podcast Player

Embed podcast episodes directly in your media kit.

**Best for:** Letting visitors hear your style and expertise

**Fields:**
- Podcast feed URL or episode embed
- Display settings

---

### Offers

Services, products, or collaboration opportunities.

**Best for:** Converting interest into business

**Fields:**
- Offer title
- Description
- Price (optional)
- CTA button

---

### Contact

Your contact information and optional contact form.

**Best for:** Making it easy for people to reach you

**Fields:**
- Email
- Phone (optional)
- Contact form toggle
- Custom message

---

### Social Links

Links to your social media profiles.

**Best for:** Connecting visitors to your other platforms

**Supported Platforms:**
- LinkedIn
- Twitter/X
- Instagram
- Facebook
- YouTube
- TikTok
- And more...

---

### Call-to-Action

A prominent button or action section.

**Best for:** Driving specific actions (book a call, download resource, etc.)

**Fields:**
- Button text
- Link URL
- Style options

---

### Booking Calendar

Embed your scheduling tool directly in your media kit.

**Best for:** Allowing visitors to book time with you instantly

**Supported Integrations:**
- Calendly
- Cal.com
- Acuity
- Custom embed
