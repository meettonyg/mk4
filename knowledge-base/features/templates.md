# Templates

Templates give you a professional starting point for your media kit. Choose a template that matches your profession and style, then customize it to make it your own.

## What is a Template?

A template is a pre-designed media kit layout with:
- **Pre-arranged components** - Professionally organized sections
- **Placeholder content** - Example text to guide you
- **Optimized layout** - Designed for your specific use case

Templates save you time and ensure you include all the essential elements for your profession.

## Using Templates

### Creating from a Template

1. Click **Create New Media Kit**
2. Browse available templates
3. Preview templates by clicking on them
4. Select your chosen template
5. Click **Use This Template**
6. Your media kit opens with the template applied

### Customizing Your Template

Once you've chosen a template:

1. **Replace placeholder content** - Update all text with your information
2. **Add your images** - Upload profile photos and relevant media
3. **Adjust components** - Add, remove, or reorder as needed
4. **Apply your brand** - Link a Brand Kit for your colors
5. **Choose a theme** - Select a visual style

### Switching Templates

Want to try a different template?

> **Note:** Switching templates will replace your current layout. Your content is preserved, but component arrangement changes.

1. Open your media kit
2. Go to **Settings** > **Template**
3. Choose a new template
4. Confirm the switch
5. Rearrange content as needed

## Template Best Practices

### Don't Skip Sections
Templates include components for good reason. Before removing a section, consider if it adds value for your audience.

### Customize the Content
Templates provide placeholder text as a guide. Always replace it with your authentic content.

### Maintain Balance
Templates are designed with visual balance. When adding or removing components, consider the overall flow.

### Use AI Tools
Generate content for template sections quickly using Guestify's AI tools:
- Biography Generator for bio sections
- Topics Generator for speaking topics
- Questions Generator for interview questions

## Creating Custom Layouts

Not finding the right template? Build from scratch:

1. Click **Create New Media Kit**
2. Select **Blank Canvas**
3. Add components one by one
4. Arrange in your preferred order
5. Save as a custom template for reuse

### Saving Custom Templates

Save your layout for future use:
1. Build your media kit
2. Go to **Settings** > **Save as Template**
3. Name your template
4. Your custom template appears in your template library
