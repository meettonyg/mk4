# What is Guestify Media Kit Builder?

Guestify Media Kit Builder (GMKB) is a powerful tool that helps you create professional, customizable media kits through an intuitive drag-and-drop interface. Whether you're a podcaster, author, speaker, consultant, or influencer, Guestify helps you showcase your expertise and build your personal brand.

## What is a Media Kit?

A media kit is a professional document or web page that presents your credentials, expertise, and value proposition to potential collaborators, podcast hosts, event organizers, and media outlets. Think of it as your professional portfolio that helps others understand who you are, what you offer, and why they should work with you.

## Who is Guestify For?

Guestify is designed for professionals who want to establish authority and grow their visibility:

- **Podcast Guests** - Create compelling profiles to get booked on podcasts
- **Authors & Writers** - Showcase your books, expertise, and speaking availability
- **Speakers & Presenters** - Present your topics, testimonials, and booking information
- **Consultants & Coaches** - Display your services, frameworks, and client results
- **Influencers & Creators** - Share your audience stats, collaboration opportunities, and brand story
- **Course Creators** - Market your educational content and expertise
- **Entrepreneurs** - Build professional profiles for networking and partnerships
