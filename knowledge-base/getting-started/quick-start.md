# Quick Start Guide

Get your professional media kit up and running in just a few minutes. This guide walks you through creating your first media kit from start to finish.

## Step 1: Create a New Media Kit

1. Log in to your Guestify account
2. Navigate to **Media Kits** in your dashboard
3. Click **Create New Media Kit**
4. Enter a name for your media kit (e.g., "Speaker Profile" or "Podcast Guest Kit")

## Step 2: Choose a Template

Select a starting template that matches your goals:

| Template | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| **Author** | Writers, book authors, thought leaders |
| **Speaker** | Keynote speakers, workshop facilitators |
| **Consultant** | Business consultants, coaches, advisors |
| **Influencer** | Social media creators, brand ambassadors |
| **Podcast Guest** | Anyone seeking podcast appearances |

Each template comes in multiple layout variants:
- **Standard** - Balanced, professional layout
- **Minimal** - Clean, focused design
- **Split** - Modern two-column approach

> **Tip:** Don't worry about picking the "perfect" template. You can customize everything after you start!

## Step 3: Add Your Information

### Profile Basics
Start by filling in your essential information:
- **Name** - Your professional name
- **Title/Role** - What you do (e.g., "Leadership Coach" or "Bestselling Author")
- **Profile Photo** - A professional headshot

### Biography
Write or generate your professional bio:
1. Click on the **Biography** component
2. Either write your own bio or click **Generate with AI**
3. Choose from Short (50 words), Medium (150 words), or Long (300 words) versions

### Topics & Expertise
Add the subjects you speak about or specialize in:
1. Click on the **Topics** component
2. Add your main topics with descriptions
3. Organize them by priority

## Step 4: Customize Components

### Adding Components
1. Click the **+ Add Component** button in the sidebar
2. Browse available components by category
3. Drag and drop components onto your media kit
4. Arrange them in your preferred order

### Editing Components
1. Click on any component to select it
2. Use the **Edit** panel on the right to modify content
3. Changes save automatically

### Removing Components
1. Select the component you want to remove
2. Click the **trash icon** or press **Delete**

## Step 5: Apply Your Branding

### Quick Theme Selection
1. Click **Theme** in the top toolbar
2. Browse available themes
3. Click to preview, then **Apply** to use

### Custom Brand Kit
For consistent branding:
1. Go to **Brand Kits** in your dashboard
2. Create a new brand kit with your colors
3. Return to your media kit and link the brand kit

## Step 6: Preview & Publish

### Preview Your Media Kit
1. Click the **Preview** button in the top toolbar
2. Check how it looks on desktop, tablet, and mobile
3. Make any final adjustments

### Publish
1. Click **Publish** when you're ready
2. Copy your unique media kit URL
3. Share it with podcast hosts, event organizers, or anyone who needs it!
