# Social Post Generator

Create engaging social media posts optimized for each platform.

## Overview

The Social Post Generator helps you create platform-specific content that drives engagement. Generate posts for:
- LinkedIn
- Twitter/X
- Instagram
- Facebook
- Threads

## Platform Best Practices

### LinkedIn
- **Length:** 1,000-1,300 characters performs best
- **Tone:** Professional but personal
- **Format:** Short paragraphs, lots of white space
- **Content:** Insights, stories, lessons learned

### Twitter/X
- **Length:** 280 characters, but shorter often better
- **Tone:** Punchy, conversational
- **Format:** Threads for longer content
- **Content:** Hot takes, tips, quotes, observations

### Instagram
- **Length:** 2,200 character limit, but lead with hook
- **Tone:** Casual, authentic
- **Format:** Carousels for value, Reels for reach
- **Content:** Behind-the-scenes, tips, inspiration

### Facebook
- **Length:** 100-250 characters for engagement
- **Tone:** Conversational, community-focused
- **Format:** Questions and stories perform well
- **Content:** Personal updates, discussions, links

## Post Types

### The Hook Post
Start with something attention-grabbing:
> "I made $0 my first year in business. Here's what I learned..."

### The Lesson Post
Share a specific insight:
> "The best networking advice I ever got: Stop trying to be interesting. Be interested."

### The Contrarian Post
Challenge conventional wisdom:
> "Unpopular opinion: Hustle culture isn't killing businesses. Lack of clarity is."

### The Story Post
Share a narrative:
> "Last week, a potential client asked for a discount. I said no. Here's why that was the best decision..."

### The List Post
Provide actionable value:
> "5 things I do every morning before checking email:
> 1. [First thing]
> 2. [Second thing]..."

### The Question Post
Drive engagement:
> "What's the best piece of business advice you've ever received?"

## How to Use

### Step 1: Choose Platform

Select your target platform—formatting and length will adapt.

### Step 2: Define Goal

What do you want this post to do?
- Drive engagement (comments, shares)
- Build authority
- Promote something
- Start a conversation

### Step 3: Enter Content

Provide:
- Your main point or message
- Any specific hook or angle
- Call to action (optional)

### Step 4: Generate and Customize

1. Click **Generate Post**
2. Review output
3. Add your voice
4. Schedule or publish

## Post Formulas

### The Hook → Insight → CTA
```
[Attention-grabbing statement]

[Expand with insight or story]

[Call to action or question]
```

### The List Format
```
[Number] things I learned about [topic]:

1. [First insight]
2. [Second insight]
3. [Third insight]

Which resonates most with you?
```

### The Before/After
```
Before: [Old way or struggle]

After: [New way or success]

The difference? [Key insight]
```

### The Story Arc
```
[Set the scene]

[Introduce conflict]

[Resolution/lesson]

[Takeaway for reader]
```

## Tips for Engagement

### Start Strong
Your first line determines if people keep reading. Make it count.

### Use White Space
Short paragraphs.

Single lines.

Easy to scan.

### Ask Questions
End with a question to prompt comments.

### Be Authentic
Share real experiences, not just polished advice.

### Engage Back
Reply to comments to boost algorithm visibility.

### Post Consistently
Regular posting builds momentum and audience expectations.
