# Offers Generator

Create compelling descriptions for your services, products, and collaboration opportunities.

## Overview

The Offers Generator helps you describe your offerings in ways that:
- Clearly communicate value
- Address what buyers care about
- Differentiate from alternatives
- Drive action

## Types of Offers

### Services
- Consulting
- Coaching
- Speaking
- Workshops
- Done-for-you services

### Products
- Books
- Courses
- Templates
- Software
- Memberships

### Collaborations
- Podcast guesting
- Speaking engagements
- Joint ventures
- Affiliate partnerships
- Media interviews

## Offer Structure

### Essential Elements

```
HEADLINE - What is it?
SUBHEADLINE - Who is it for?
PROBLEM - What pain does it solve?
SOLUTION - How does it solve it?
BENEFITS - What do they get?
PROOF - Why should they believe you?
CTA - What should they do next?
```

## How to Use

### Step 1: Define Your Offer

Enter:
- What you're offering
- Who it's for

- Price point (optional)

### Step 2: Generate Description

1. Click **Generate Offer**
2. Review the description
3. Customize for your voice
4. Add specific details

### Step 3: Place in Media Kit

Add to your Offers component or dedicated page.

## Writing Compelling Offers

### Lead with Transformation
Don't describe features—describe results:

**Feature:** "8 video modules"
**Transformation:** "Learn the system that 200+ founders used to scale"

### Address Objections
Anticipate and answer concerns:
- "Limited to 5 clients" → Addresses fear of getting lost in the crowd
- "Typical results: 40%" → Addresses "will this work?"
- "90-day money back" → Addresses risk

### Use Specificity
**Vague:** "Get better results"
**Specific:** "40% revenue increase within 6 months"

### Include Social Proof
- Client results
- Testimonials
- Media features
- Numbers

### Clear Call to Action
Tell them exactly what to do:
- "Apply for a fit call"
- "Download the free template"
- "Book a discovery session"

## Offers for Your Media Kit

### What to Include
Your media kit should highlight:
1. **Your signature offer** - Main service/product
2. **Speaking/guesting** - Make it easy to book you
3. **Quick wins** - Lower-commitment options

### What to Exclude
Don't include:
- Too many options (overwhelm)
- Complex pricing tables
- Offers that aren't ready
- Anything that needs extensive explanation

## Pricing Display

### Options
1. **Show pricing** - Good for productized services
2. **Show range** - "Starting at $X"
3. **No pricing** - "Apply for pricing" (custom work)

### When to Show Price
- Simple, clear offering
- You want to qualify by budget
- Competitors show pricing

### When to Hide Price
- Custom/complex work
- High-ticket services
- Negotiable terms
