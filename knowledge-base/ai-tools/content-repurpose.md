# Content Repurpose Tool

Transform one piece of content into multiple formats to maximize reach and impact.

## Overview

The Content Repurpose Tool helps you get more mileage from your best content by adapting it for different platforms and formats. One podcast interview can become:
- Blog post
- LinkedIn posts
- Twitter thread
- Email newsletter
- Short video clips
- Audiograms
- Quote graphics

## Why Repurpose Content?

- **Efficiency** - Create once, use many times
- **Reach** - Different platforms, different audiences
- **Reinforcement** - Repetition builds recognition
- **Testing** - See what resonates where

## The Repurposing Hierarchy

### Long-Form Original (Most Effort)
- Podcast episode
- Keynote speech
- Workshop
- Long video
- Book chapter

### Medium-Form Adaptations
- Blog posts
- Newsletter issues
- LinkedIn articles
- YouTube videos

### Short-Form Snippets
- Social media posts
- Quote graphics
- Short videos/Reels
- Twitter threads
- Email teasers

## How to Use

### Step 1: Start with Your Best Content

Choose content that:
- Performed well originally
- Contains evergreen insights
- Has multiple teachable moments
- Resonates with your audience

### Step 2: Identify Repurposing Opportunities

From a single podcast interview, extract:
- 1 blog post
- 3-5 social media posts
- 1 Twitter thread
- 5-10 quote graphics
- 3 email teasers
- 1 newsletter edition

### Step 3: Transform

1. Paste your original content
2. Select target format
3. Click **Repurpose**
4. Review and customize for platform

## Repurposing Examples

### Podcast → Blog Post
- Use the transcript as a starting point
- Add structure with subheadings
- Expand key points
- Add links and resources

### Keynote → LinkedIn Posts
- Extract one key insight per post
- Make it actionable
- Add a hook and call to action

### Blog Post → Twitter Thread
- Break into 5-12 tweets
- Make each tweet standalone
- Number them (1/, 2/, etc.)
- End with a summary and CTA

### Interview → Quote Graphics
- Pull memorable one-liners
- Add your photo
- Use your brand colors

### Video → Audiogram
- Extract best 60-second clips
- Add captions
- Include waveform visualization

## Platform-Specific Considerations

### LinkedIn
- Professional tone
- Longer posts perform well (1,000-1,300 characters)
- Stories and lessons
- End with engagement prompt

### Twitter/X
- Punchy and concise
- Threads for depth
- Strong first tweet
- Easy to retweet

### Instagram
- Visual-first
- Carousel posts for value
- Stories for personality
- Reels for reach

### Email
- Personal connection
- One main idea
- Clear action

### Blog
- SEO considerations
- Comprehensive coverage
- Scannable format

## Repurposing Workflow

### Weekly Workflow
1. **Monday** - Identify content to repurpose
2. **Tuesday** - Transform into new formats
3. **Wednesday-Friday** - Schedule across platforms
4. **Ongoing** - Track performance, refine approach

### Batch Processing
1. Collect all original content from the month
2. Dedicate one day to repurposing
3. Create all variations at once
4. Schedule using automation tools

## Tips for Effective Repurposing

### Adapt, Don't Just Copy
Each platform has its own culture. Adapt tone and format.

### Update for Context
Add platform-specific hooks and calls to action.

### Maintain Quality
Repurposed content should feel native, not recycled.

### Track What Works
Note which repurposed content performs best where.

### Space It Out
Don't publish the same message everywhere simultaneously.

## Content That Repurposes Well

- Frameworks and processes
- Surprising insights
- Contrarian opinions
- Personal stories
- Data and statistics
- Quotable wisdom
- Step-by-step guides
