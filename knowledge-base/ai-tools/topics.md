# Topics Generator

Generate speaking topics and areas of expertise that showcase your knowledge and attract the right opportunities.

## Overview

The Topics Generator helps you identify and articulate the subjects you can speak or write about. Well-defined topics help:
- Podcast hosts understand what to interview you about
- Event organizers match you to their audience
- Content creators plan what to feature
- Visitors understand your expertise areas

## What Makes Good Topics?

Good topics are:
- **Specific** - Not "marketing" but "email marketing for e-commerce"
- **Outcome-focused** - Promise a transformation or insight
- **Differentiated** - Show your unique angle
- **Relevant** - Match current trends and audience needs

## How to Use

### Step 1: Identify Your Expertise

Ask yourself:
- What do people consistently ask me about?
- What do I have unique experience in?
- What trends am I ahead on?
- What controversial opinions do I hold?

### Step 2: Generate Topics

1. Enter your expertise areas and target audience
2. Click **Generate Topics**
3. Review and select the most compelling
4. Refine titles and descriptions

### Step 3: Add Depth

For each topic, define:
- A compelling title
- A brief description
- 3-5 key talking points
- Who would benefit

## Topic Formulas

### The How-To
> "How to [achieve outcome] Without [common obstacle]"
- "How to Scale to $10M Without Burning Out"

### The Contrarian
> "Why [common belief] is Wrong About [topic]"
- "Why Everything You Know About SEO is Wrong"

### The Framework
> "The [Number] [Components] of [Outcome]"
- "The 5 Pillars of Unstoppable Confidence"

### The Secret/Hidden
> "The Hidden [Truth/Secret/Key] to [Outcome]"
- "The Hidden Key to Building Million-Dollar Relationships"

### The Mistakes
> "[Number] Mistakes [Audience] Makes With [Topic]"
- "7 Mistakes New Managers Make in Their First 90 Days"

### The Trend
> "The Future of [Topic]: What [Audience] Needs to Know"
- "The Future of Remote Work: What Leaders Need to Know Now"

## Organizing Your Topics

### Primary Topics (3-5)
Your main areas of expertise. These should be:
- Well-developed with lots of material
- Closely tied to your services
- In high demand

### Secondary Topics (3-5)
Related areas you can speak on. These:
- Support your primary topics
- Show range and depth
- Attract different audiences

### Emerging Topics (1-3)
New areas you're developing:
- Trending subjects
- New frameworks
- Fresh perspectives

## Tips for Compelling Topics

### Lead with Benefit
**Weak:** "Email Marketing Best Practices"
**Strong:** "Email Marketing Secrets That Generated $50M in Sales"

### Be Specific
**Weak:** "Leadership"
**Strong:** "First-Time Manager Survival Guide: Your First 90 Days"

### Add Credibility
**Good:** "Content Marketing Strategies"
**Better:** "The Content Marketing Strategy That Built a 500K Audience"

### Include Your Angle
What's YOUR take on this topic? What makes it different?
