# Podcast Details Extractor

Extract key information from podcast episodes for your media kit and records.

## Overview

The Podcast Details Extractor helps you quickly pull important information from podcast episodes you've appeared on:
- Episode titles and dates
- Show information
- Key talking points
- Quotes and soundbites
- Links and resources

## Use Cases

### Building Your Appearances List
Quickly capture details from each podcast appearance for your media kit.

### Creating Content
Extract quotes and talking points for repurposing into other content.

### Tracking Your Reach
Document audience size and download numbers for credibility.

### Follow-Up
Gather information for thank-you notes and relationship building.

## Information Extracted

### Episode Details
- Show name
- Episode title
- Episode number
- Publication date
- Episode URL
- Download/listen link

### Show Information
- Host name(s)
- Show description
- Audience size (if available)
- Show topic/niche

### Content Details
- Topics discussed
- Key talking points
- Questions asked
- Your main insights

### Quotes & Highlights
- Memorable quotes
- Key moments
- Timestamps of best sections

### Links & Resources
- Resources you mentioned
- Links shared
- Your call-to-action

## How to Use

### Step 1: Provide Episode Information

Options:
- Paste episode URL
- Enter show notes
- Paste transcript
- Upload audio

### Step 2: Extract

1. Click **Extract Details**
2. Review extracted information
3. Verify accuracy
4. Add any missing details

### Step 3: Save to Media Kit

Add the episode to your Interviews component with:
- Show name and logo
- Episode title
- Date
- Link

## Tips for Accurate Extraction

### Provide Good Source Material
The better your input, the better the extraction:
- Full transcript is best
- Episode URL helps gather show metadata
- Show notes provide key details

### Verify Before Saving
Always double-check:
- Correct show name and host
- Accurate episode date
- Working links
- Correct spelling of names

### Add Context
Include information that isn't in the episode:
- How you were introduced to the host
- Audience feedback received
- Performance metrics (if known)
