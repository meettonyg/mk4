# Biography Generator

Create professional biographies perfect for media kits, speaker profiles, and author pages. Generate bios in multiple lengths optimized for different use cases.

## Overview

The Biography Generator helps you craft compelling professional bios that showcase your expertise, achievements, and unique value. Get polished, professional content tailored to your needs in seconds.

## Bio Lengths

### Short Bio (50 words)
Perfect for:
- Social media profiles (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Quick introductions
- Email signatures
- Conference badges

### Medium Bio (150 words)
Ideal for:
- Speaker introductions
- Media kit primary bio
- Author bylines
- Podcast guest profiles

### Long Bio (300 words)
Comprehensive bio for:
- Detailed website profiles
- Book jackets
- Press releases
- Award nominations

## How to Use

### Step 1: Enter Your Information

**Required:**
- Your name

**Recommended for best results:**
- WHO do you help?
- WHAT result do you deliver?
- WHEN do they need you?
- HOW do you do it?
- Credentials and achievements (WHERE)
- Your mission (WHY)

### Step 2: Generate Your Bios

1. Click **Generate Bio**
2. Select which length(s) to generate
3. Review the generated options
4. Each length shows 2-5 variations

### Step 3: Refine and Select

For each length:
1. Review all variations
2. Click **Refine** to improve any version
3. Use **Lock** to mark your favorite
4. Continue refining until perfect

### Step 4: Save to Profile

1. Click **Save to Profile**
2. Your bios are saved to your media kit
3. Biography components auto-update with your content

## The Formula

A powerful biography combines your Authority Hook and Impact Intro:

```
I help [WHO] achieve [WHAT] when [WHEN] through [HOW].
I've [WHERE].
My mission is to [WHY].
```

## Tips for Better Bios

### Be Specific
Instead of: "Helped many companies grow"
Try: "Helped 200+ companies increase revenue by an average of 40%"

### Show Results
Instead of: "I'm a marketing expert"
Try: "My clients typically see 3x ROI within 90 days"

### Include Credentials
Mention:
- Degrees and certifications
- Publications and features
- Speaking experience
- Awards and recognition

### Choose the Right Point of View

**Third Person** (Recommended for most use cases)
> "Sarah Chen helps ambitious entrepreneurs..."

**First Person** (Better for personal websites, social media)
> "I help ambitious entrepreneurs..."

### End with Personality
Add a personal touch:
- Hobbies or interests
- Location
- Fun fact

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which point of view should I use?
Third person is standard for professional media kits and speaker profiles. First person works better for personal websites and social media.

### Can I edit the generated bio?
Absolutely! Use the refinement feedback loop to iterate on any variation, or copy and edit manually.

### What's the best length for a speaker bio?
Medium (150 words) works well for most speaking applications. Short bios are great for quick intros, while long bios suit detailed media kits.

### How does slot-based generation work?
We only generate variations for the length you're actively working on. This saves processing and lets you focus on one bio at a time.

### Can I have different bios for different contexts?
Yes! Create variations for:
- Speaking engagements
- Consulting inquiries
- Media interviews
- Book/author contexts
